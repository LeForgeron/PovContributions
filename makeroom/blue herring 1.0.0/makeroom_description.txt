Creates a low detail room interior for use as a setting or background.  The walls, ceiling, and floor can be individually textured.  Optionally the following features can be easily added to the room,

- Windows, rectangular or elliptical, 5 styles each, with or without glass.
- Door openings, 2 styles.
- Baseboards, shape can be customized.
- Crown molding, shape can be customized.

Textures can be assigned to any and all of these elements.
